How many students receive a scholarship covering 100% of their fees?

‹
Definition
History
›
A grant or payment made to support a student's education, awarded on the basis of academic or other achievement, that covers a full academic year for full payment of fees. For rankings purposes, this is for fees only. The fund may come from the university, a private company, or a philanthropic organization.
